Beef Kofta Recipe

Beautiful Recipe. Serves 4 and can be frozen.

Ingredients

  • Avocado oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 750ml beef stock
  • 225g butternut squash, peeled & chopped
  • 400g minced beef
  • 10g fresh coriander, chopped
  • salt & pepper
  • 75g natural yogurt

Instructions

  1. Spray a saucepan with avocado oil, add the onion and cook over medium heat for 4-5 mins, until soft and beginning to brown.
  2. Add 2/3 of the garlic and the curry powder. Continue to cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add the tomato puree, stirring well. Then add the stock.
  4. Add the butternut squash, stir and bring to the boil. Then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, mix the minced beef with the remaining garlic, 1/2 the chopped coriander and salt & pepper. Divide into ~12 or more pieces and form into meatballs.
  6. Add oil to a frying pan and add the meatballs. Fry over med-high heat, turning frequently. Fry until brown and cooked through. Remove and place to the side.
  7. When the butternut squash is soft remove from the heat and blitz with a stick blender until smooth.
  8. Add the meatballs, stir well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Remove from the heat and serve with the yogurt and coriander poured on top.
